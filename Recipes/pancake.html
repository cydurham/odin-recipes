Souffle Pancake

Description

Super thick pancakes like in anime.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 & 1/2 tbsp Whole milk
  • 1/4 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup cake flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Tbsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp neutral oil (for greasing the pan)
  • 2 Tbsp water (for steaming)
